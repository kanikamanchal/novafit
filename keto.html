Metabolic Science

KETO PEAK shifts your metabolism from glucose-dependence to ketone utilization. By drastically reducing carbs and moderating protein, your liver produces ketones — an ultra-clean fuel that enhances endurance and mental performance. Our precision protocol avoids common pitfalls (keto flu, electrolyte imbalance) with strategic mineral support.

Fat Adaptation Timeline

Phase 1 (days 1-3): glycogen depletion.
Phase 2 (days 4-10): rising ketones, possible transition symptoms.
Phase 3 (week 2+): full metabolic flexibility, skyrocketing energy.

The Science of Timing

FAST & FUEL uses the 16:8 protocol (16h fast, 8h eating window). Fasting triggers norepinephrine release, increases lipolysis, and upregulates sirtuins. Nutrient timing within the window maximizes muscle protein synthesis and glycogen replenishment. NOVA's adaptive algorithm personalizes your fasting schedule.

Ideal Fasting Windows

Morning fast: 8pm - 12pm next day (skip breakfast).
Alternative: 10am - 6pm feeding window.
Advanced: 18:6 or 20:4 protocols.
NOVA app tracks fasting glucose & ketones.

Fasting Aids

Black coffee, green tea, electrolyte water, apple cider vinegar. Breaking fast: start with protein + healthy fats to avoid blood sugar spike.

🥩 Best Breaking-Fast Foods

  • Eggs / Egg whites
  • Greek yogurt (high protein)
  • Lean meat or fish
  • Protein shake + berries
  • Avocado, nuts

🌱 Fasting-Friendly Beverages

  • Black coffee, green tea
  • Electrolyte water (no sugar)
  • Apple cider vinegar diluted
  • Herbal teas (peppermint, chamomile)

⚠️ What to Avoid

  • Sugary drinks during fast
  • Large insulin spikes when breaking fast
  • Processed snacks in feeding window
  • Overeating to compensate

PROTEIN FIRST

High-protein blueprint for maximal muscle repair, lean mass gain, and accelerated recovery — precision amino acid timing.
Protein: 35-40% Carbs: 35-40% Fats: 20-25% 4-6 meals/day

Muscle First Philosophy

  • 1.6-2.2g protein per kg bodyweight
  • Leucine-enriched meals for MPS trigger
  • Nighttime casein for overnight repair
  • Reduced muscle breakdown (anti-catabolic)
  • Faster return to training intensity

The Science of Protein Timing

PROTEIN FIRST leverages the anabolic window: 20-40g high-quality protein every 3-4 hours. We emphasize essential amino acids (EAAs) and leucine threshold (>2.5g per meal). Combined with strategic carb cycling, this protocol maximizes muscle protein synthesis and minimizes fatigue.

Recovery Phases

Immediate post-workout: fast-absorbing whey or hydrolyzed plant protein.
Mid-day: whole food sources (chicken, fish, legumes).
Before sleep: micellar casein or Greek yogurt.
NOVA recovery tracker monitors nitrogen balance.

Key Supplements

Whey isolate, EAAs, creatine monohydrate, digestive enzymes, and magnesium glycinate for deep sleep recovery. All integrated into the NOVA app for smart reminders.
